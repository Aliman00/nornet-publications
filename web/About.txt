NorNet is a national infrastructure targeting the networking research community in Norway and beyond. The goal of NorNet is to provide a flexible platform for supporting a wide range of experiments and for testing new solutions that are not ready for deployment in operative networks.

NorNet is financed by Simula Resaerch Laboratory and the Research Council of Norway through their <a href="http://www.forskningsradet.no/prognett-infrastruktur/">INFRASTRUKTUR program</a> (project number 208798/F50).

For questions or inquiries, please contact <a title="amund's homepage" href="http://simula.no/people/amundk">Dr. Amund Kvalbein</a>.
